# Privacy Policy

**Reentry to Recovery Content Library**
**Last updated: June 1, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

The Reentry to Recovery Content Library ("the Platform," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by a non-profit organization committed to supporting incarcerated and returning individuals in the United States through free educational resources. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who can access it, and how we protect it.

By creating an account or using the Platform, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Use of the Platform is voluntary. This policy applies to all users who access the Platform through a participating correctional facility.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Username** — A unique identifier you choose (letters, numbers, and underscores)
- **First and last name**
- **Facility** — The correctional facility you are enrolled at, determined by the URL through which you access the Platform
- **Inmate PIN** — A numeric PIN associated with your facility enrollment, used to verify your identity on your device
- **Password** — Stored as a one-way cryptographic hash. We cannot read or recover your password.
- **Security questions and answers** — Two questions and answers of your choosing, used only for password recovery. Answers are encrypted using a server-side secret before storage — we cannot read your answers.
- **Account creation date**

### 2.2 Content Engagement Data

As you use the Platform, we automatically record:

- **Which content items you open** and when
- **Completion status** — whether you have completed each item, and the date of completion
- **Media progress** — for video and audio content, how far through you watched or listened (expressed as a percentage)
- **PDF reading progress** — an estimated reading percentage based on time spent in the PDF viewer
- **Session time** — how many seconds you actively engaged with each piece of content in each session, measured by detecting real activity (scrolling, clicking, playing) and stopping after 90 seconds of inactivity
- **Category views** — which program categories you visit

### 2.3 Ratings

If you voluntarily rate content as "Helpful" or "Not Helpful," we record:

- Which item you rated
- The rating you gave (helpful or not helpful)
- The date of the rating

### 2.4 Bookmarks

If you save resources using the bookmark feature, we record which content items you have bookmarked and when.

### 2.5 Login History

We record the calendar dates on which you log in to your account. This is used to calculate your day streak on your dashboard.

### 2.6 Achievements

We record which milestones you have earned (e.g., "First Resource," "7-Day Streak") and the date each was earned.

### 2.7 Technical Information

When you use the Platform, our hosting infrastructure may automatically log standard technical data such as the device type and browser used to access the Platform. This data is used for security and infrastructure purposes only and is not linked to your personal account for analytics purposes.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

**To provide the service:**
- Display your personal progress dashboard, including completion rings, stat cards, category progress, bookmarks, ratings, and achievements
- Allow you to reset your password using your security questions
- Remember where you left off in video and audio content

**For program reporting and improvement:**
- Generate aggregate reports for facility administrators showing overall engagement, completion rates, time spent, and content ratings across all users — without identifying individual users in those reports
- Produce individual user progress reports accessible to facility administrators and our non-profit staff for program oversight, case notes, and grant reporting
- Identify which content is most and least effective based on aggregate engagement data
- Report program outcomes to funders and grant agencies using anonymized or aggregate statistics

**For security:**
- Verify your identity at login and ensure only authorized users access accounts
- Detect and prevent abuse or unauthorized access to the Platform
- Apply rate limits to protect against automated attacks on account creation and password recovery

---

## 4. Who Can See Your Information

### You
Your personal dashboard shows your own progress, time spent, ratings, bookmarks, and achievements. Only you can see your own dashboard when logged in.

### Facility Administrators
Staff accounts at your participating facility ("facilityUser" role) can access:
- Aggregate engagement reports for their facility (total users, completion rates, time spent, rating counts — no individual attribution for ratings in aggregate reports)
- Individual user progress reports showing your completed items, time spent per item, media progress percentages, bookmark status, and your specific ratings per item

Facility administrators cannot access your password, security question answers, or the accounts of users at other facilities.

### Non-Profit Staff
Administrators and contributors at our non-profit organization can access:
- All data available to facility administrators, across all facilities
- Individual user progress reports for any user, for program evaluation, technical support, and grant reporting
- Aggregate analytics across all facilities and programs

Non-profit staff cannot access passwords or security question answers in readable form, as these are cryptographically protected.

### Other Users
Other users of the Platform cannot see your account, your progress, your ratings, or any other personal information.

### Third-Party Service Providers
We use the following third-party services to operate the Platform:

- **Supabase** — Our database and authentication provider. Your account data, content progress, and all platform data is stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure. Supabase acts as a data processor under our direction and does not independently use your data.
- **Cloudflare** — Our content delivery and deployment infrastructure. Cloudflare processes web traffic to deliver the Platform and provides security services such as DDoS protection.

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use advertising networks or allow third-party tracking on this Platform.

## 5. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Measures we employ include:

- **Encrypted passwords** — Passwords are hashed using industry-standard cryptography before storage and are never stored or transmitted in plain text
- **Encrypted security answers** — Security question answers are hashed using a server-side secret key before storage and cannot be recovered by anyone, including our staff
- **Row-Level Security** — Our database enforces access controls at the row level, meaning each user can only read and write their own data through the application
- **HTTPS encryption** — All traffic between your device and our servers is encrypted in transit via HTTPS, enforced by Cloudflare
- **Rate limiting** — Sensitive endpoints (account creation, password reset, login) are rate-limited to prevent automated attacks
- **Shared device protections** — Because the Platform is used on shared facility tablets, we require a facility-specific PIN to log in and encourage users to sign out after each session. We also prevent a user's PIN from being used to access a different user's account

No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please notify your facility's program coordinator immediately.

---

## 6. Data Retention

**While your account is active:** All account information, content progress, ratings, bookmarks, login history, and achievements are retained for as long as your account exists.

**After account deletion:** If your account is deleted at your request or by a facility administrator, your personally identifiable information will be removed from our active database. Aggregated and anonymized statistics (such as total completion counts per content item or per facility) may be retained indefinitely as they cannot be traced back to any individual.

**Inactive accounts:** We do not automatically delete accounts due to inactivity. Your progress remains available if you return to the Platform.

---

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

**Access:** You can view your own progress, ratings, and bookmarks at any time on your personal dashboard.

**Correction:** If your name or other account information is incorrect, you can update it in the Account Settings section of your dashboard.

**Deletion:** To request deletion of your account and associated personal data, please speak with your facility's program coordinator, who can submit a deletion request on your behalf.

**Ratings and bookmarks:** You can change or remove any rating or bookmark at any time directly within the Platform.

Please note that because the Platform is accessed through a correctional facility's infrastructure, certain requests may need to go through your facility's program coordinator rather than directly to us.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Platform is intended exclusively for adults who are incarcerated at participating correctional facilities. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has created an account on the Platform, please contact us through your facility's program coordinator.
